About Rahisisha Tech

We're a forward-thinking technology company dedicated to simplifying business operations through innovative AI solutions and cutting-edge development practices across Africa.

Our Story

Founded with a vision to democratize AI technology across Africa, Rahisisha Tech emerged from the recognition that businesses needed accessible, practical AI solutions to compete in the digital age.

2021

The Beginning

Started as a small team of passionate developers and AI enthusiasts in Nairobi, Kenya, with a mission to make AI accessible to African businesses.

2022

First Major Success

Delivered our first AI automation solution that helped a local manufacturing company reduce processing time by 60% and save $50,000 annually.

2023

Expansion & Growth

Expanded our services to include comprehensive web development, mobile apps, and digital marketing, serving over 500 clients across East Africa.

2024

AI Training Initiative

Launched our AI training programs to empower African businesses with the knowledge and skills to leverage AI tools effectively.

Our Mission

To democratize AI technology and make it accessible to businesses of all sizes across Africa, enabling them to compete globally while solving local challenges through innovative technology solutions.

Our Vision

To be the leading catalyst for business transformation in Africa through intelligent technology solutions, creating a future where every business can harness the power of AI for growth and success.

A Message from Our Founder

"When we started Rahisisha Tech, we had a simple but powerful vision: to make AI technology accessible and practical for African businesses. We've seen firsthand how the right technology can transform operations, increase efficiency, and drive growth.

Our name 'Rahisisha' means 'to make easier' in Swahili, and that's exactly what we do. We take complex AI and technology concepts and make them simple, practical, and valuable for our clients.

Today, I'm proud to say we've helped over 500 businesses across East Africa leverage technology to achieve their goals. But we're just getting started. The future of business in Africa is digital, and we're here to make that transition as smooth and successful as possible."

John Mwangi

Founder & CEO, Rahisisha Tech
